Department Of Computer Engineering

Computer Engineering Department (CPED) at Khwaja Fareed University of Engineering and Information Technology (KFUEIT) is established in 2017. Department of Computer Engineering enjoys newly purpose built infra structure, with faculty offices, teaching rooms, lecture theatres, and laboratories. The department has well experienced teaching staff from diverse backgrounds and practised an Outcome Based Education at CPE to conform to Washington Accord. ESPICE got held every semester in which students from different departments present their works under the supervision of Computer Engineering Faculty Members. CPED offers specialization in:

  • Computer Engineering

  • Software Engineering

The department mission is the discovery, dissemination and application of knowledge by offering degree programs at the undergraduate and graduate levels and pursues a broad agenda of research and creative activities and to become a globally recognized Computer Engineering Department that will have an overwhelming impact on society through continual innovation in education, research, creativity, and entrepreneurship.
